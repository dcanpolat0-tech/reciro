# Nereye Screenshot Guide

Last updated: July 13, 2026

Use clean sample data. Do not show private receipts, real addresses, phone numbers, or card details.

## Recommended iPhone Screenshots

Apple usually expects screenshots for current iPhone display sizes. Capture them from a real iPhone or simulator after the final build.

## Screens To Capture

1. Home
   - Show monthly total, remaining money, receipt count, and recent receipts.
   - Caption: `See your spending clearly`

2. Add Receipt
   - Show the green add receipt button and camera/gallery choices.
   - Caption: `Add a receipt in seconds`

3. AI Review
   - Show the receipt image, store, date, total, category, and editable products.
   - Caption: `Review AI results before saving`

4. Receipt Detail
   - Show a saved receipt with photo and products.
   - Caption: `Keep every receipt in one place`

5. Reports
   - Show category and store breakdown.
   - Caption: `Understand where your money goes`

6. Products
   - Show most purchased products sorted by quantity.
   - Caption: `See what you buy most`

7. Settings
   - Show language, currency, income, feedback, and premium.
   - Caption: `Customize Nereye for daily use`

## Screenshot Rules

- Use English for the first App Store release screenshots.
- Use a realistic currency, preferably EUR for the first market.
- Use realistic but fake stores and products.
- Do not use blurry receipt photos.
- Make sure the bottom menu is not covering important buttons.
- Battery/time bar should look clean.
